This program is a simple game where the computer tries to guess a word you give it, one character at a time. It randomly picks letters, numbers, punctuation, or spaces to guess each letter in the word until it gets it right. As it guesses, the word is gradually filled in with the correct letters, and it simulates a typing effect to show the progress.
